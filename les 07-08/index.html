Develop a project in a multidisciplinary European team!

What

Mind the Gap is an Intensive Programme which brings together Turkish, Danish, Finnish, Portuguese, Belgian and Spanish students and teachers with art and technology backgrounds. Together they will form international multidisciplinary teams to develop web app prototypes around the theme of European Citizenship.

read more

Why

It's a cliché, but some cliché's are true: designers and developers speak a different language. In this Intensive Programme we'll use the principle of "show don't tell" to eliminate common misunderstandings. We'll mend the gap by using the prototype as a common language.
